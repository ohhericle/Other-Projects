ABOUT US

Lifestyle Shoes is a multinational corporation, founded and headquartered in San Diego, California, that designs and manufactures shoes to everyone in the world. It is the largest sport shoe manufacturer in the United States, and the second largest in the world, after Nike.

About the Founder

Lifestyle Shoes was founded in 2019 by Eric Le. Eric was born and raised in San Diego, California. He is currently on his fifth year at San Diego State University studying Management Information System with a minor in Computer Science. He founded Lifestyle Shoes as a way to improve peoples everyday lives wheater or not it involves hiking up large cliffs or simply walking to your destination.

Future Plans

Future plans for the company involves 3D printing. The vision is for the company to produce shoes with customizable insoles specifically for consumers’ feet using the layering technology. Once we master production offsite, we will bring it right to local stores who have 3D printers. This is the company’s attempt to modernize production, and the in-store focus will help appease retailers around the world.
